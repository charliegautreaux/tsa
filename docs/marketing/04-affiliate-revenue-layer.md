# 04 — Affiliate & Partnership Revenue Layer

**Site**: PreBoard.ai | **Date**: 2026-03-26

---

## Why Affiliate Revenue Is Uniquely Powerful for PreBoard.ai

PreBoard.ai users are in a rare psychological state: they're actively anxious about airport security wait times and highly receptive to solutions that reduce that anxiety. This creates natural, non-intrusive affiliate placement opportunities for products that directly solve the user's problem. Unlike generic travel blogs where affiliate links feel forced, PreBoard.ai's context makes them genuinely helpful.

---

## 1. TSA PreCheck Enrollment

### The Opportunity

Every user viewing a 30+ minute wait time is a warm lead for TSA PreCheck. The pitch writes itself: "Tired of these lines? PreCheck members wait an average of 5 minutes."

### Enrollment Providers

TSA contracts with three enrollment providers: **IDEMIA**, **CLEAR**, and **Telos**.

- **IDEMIA**: Largest network (650+ enrollment centers). Enrollment costs $76.75 for 5 years.
- **CLEAR**: Also offers TSA PreCheck enrollment alongside their CLEAR+ product. $78 for PreCheck.
- **Telos**: Smaller network, may present travel deals during enrollment.

### Affiliate Program Status

As of 2026, TSA PreCheck enrollment does **not** have a formal public affiliate program through IDEMIA or Telos. However, there are workarounds:

1. **Travelpayouts**: Aggregated travel affiliate platform that occasionally offers TSA-adjacent deals.
2. **Credit card affiliate programs**: The real money is in promoting travel credit cards that **reimburse** TSA PreCheck fees ($85–$100 statement credit). Cards like Chase Sapphire Reserve, Amex Platinum, Capital One Venture X all offer this. Credit card CPAs range from **$50–$200+ per approved application**.
3. **Content-driven monetization**: Write "How to Get TSA PreCheck" guide → rank for 40K monthly searches → monetize with display ads ($20+ RPM for this keyword) + credit card affiliate links.

### Integration into PreBoard.ai

- **Airport detail page**: When showing a wait time > 15 minutes, display a contextual callout: "PreCheck members typically wait under 5 minutes at this checkpoint. Learn how to enroll →"
- **Guide pages**: Dedicated section on each airport guide about PreCheck lane availability and nearest enrollment center
- **Blog content**: "How to Get TSA PreCheck" article as a cornerstone affiliate page

### Estimated Revenue

- At 50K monthly sessions, ~5% click-through to PreCheck/credit card content = 2,500 clicks
- Credit card affiliate conversion rate: ~1–2% of clicks → 25–50 approvals/month
- Average CPA for travel credit cards: $75–$150
- **Estimated monthly revenue: $1,875–$7,500**

## 2. CLEAR+ Membership

### The Opportunity

CLEAR+ ($209/year, or $169 depending on current pricing) lets members skip the ID verification line and go straight to screening. When paired with PreCheck, it's the fastest possible path through security.

### Affiliate Program

- CLEAR has run affiliate programs through **FlexOffers** and **CJ Affiliate** in the past, though availability fluctuates.
- Commission model: Typically $15–$40 per membership signup (when program is active).
- Cookie duration: ~120 days (historically via FlexOffers).
- Credit card path is more reliable: Amex Platinum reimburses CLEAR+ membership ($209 credit). Promoting the Amex Platinum with "Get CLEAR+ free" angle is high-converting.

### Integration into PreBoard.ai

- **Airport detail page**: Show CLEAR lane availability per checkpoint (data already in schema: `has_clear` boolean). When CLEAR is available, add: "This checkpoint has a CLEAR lane. Members skip to the front →"
- **Comparison content**: "TSA PreCheck vs CLEAR: Which Should You Get?" — one of the highest-value affiliate articles in the travel space

### Estimated Revenue

- Lower volume than PreCheck (CLEAR is less well-known)
- At 50K sessions: ~1,500 clicks to CLEAR content, 1% conversion = 15 signups/month
- At $25 average commission: **~$375/month direct**
- Credit card CPA uplift (Amex Platinum angle): additional **$500–$1,500/month**

## 3. Travel Credit Cards (Highest CPA Opportunity)

### Why This Is the Biggest Revenue Lever

Travel credit cards pay the highest affiliate commissions of any product category relevant to PreBoard.ai:

| Card | Network/Platform | CPA | Cookie | Angle for PreBoard.ai |
|------|-----------------|-----|--------|-----------------------|
| Chase Sapphire Reserve | CJ Affiliate / Bankrate | $75–$150 | 30 days | "Reimburses TSA PreCheck + Global Entry" |
| Amex Platinum | Amex affiliate | $100–$200 | 30 days | "Free CLEAR+ membership + PreCheck credit" |
| Capital One Venture X | CJ Affiliate | $75–$150 | 30 days | "TSA PreCheck/Global Entry credit included" |
| Amex Gold | Amex affiliate | $50–$100 | 30 days | General travel rewards |
| Chase Sapphire Preferred | CJ / Bankrate | $50–$100 | 30 days | Entry-level premium travel card |

### Integration Strategy

- **"Cards That Cover TSA PreCheck"** — dedicated comparison page. This is an extremely high-intent keyword.
- **Contextual CTA on airport pages**: "Many travel cards reimburse TSA PreCheck enrollment — see which ones →"
- **Blog content**: "Best Travel Credit Cards for Airport Perks (2026)" — list article with affiliate links

### Compliance Note

Credit card affiliate content must include disclosure: "This page contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission if you apply and are approved." FTC guidelines require clear, conspicuous disclosure near the top of the page.
