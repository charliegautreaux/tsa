## Sensitivity Analysis

### What Moves the Needle Most?

| Lever | +50% Impact on Annual Revenue |
|-------|-------------------------------|
| **Traffic growth** | +50% (linear) — most important lever |
| **RPM optimization** (video ads, better placements) | +25–35% |
| **Affiliate conversion rate** | +10–15% |
| **Content page mix** (more guide pages vs data pages) | +15–20% |
| **Seasonal optimization** (heavy content push pre-Q4) | +10–15% |

**Takeaway**: Traffic is king. The single most important thing PreBoard.ai can do for ad revenue is grow organic sessions through the SEO content strategy (doc 03). RPM optimization is the second lever — primarily through network tier upgrades and video ad enablement.
